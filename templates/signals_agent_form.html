📖 How Signals Agents Work

  • Signal Discovery: The sales agent calls your signals agent's get_signals MCP tool with the buyer's brief
  • Signal Transformation: Retrieved signals are transformed into custom products with enhanced targeting
  • Product Enhancement: Signal-enhanced products are combined with regular database products
  • Multi-Agent Support: If multiple agents are configured, signals are aggregated from all enabled agents
  • Priority Configuration: Priority and max signal products are configured per-product, not per-agent
  • Testing: After saving, use the test button on the agents list to verify connectivity
